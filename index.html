Global Destination Application!

Are you tired of work and ready to go on the vacation of your life? Try out our application and you can see the marvelous trips that await!



The Global Destination Application that I wrote was initially very limited in its capabilities. The starting form of this application would only open up and give a basic slideshow of the destination locations, with everything hard-coded in python. I decided to take this small application and expand it with a larger one written in C# for Windows PC's. My experience with C# was limited to small project that I had done on my own, and my experience with gui's in C# was with the Windows Forms. For this project however, I chose to use the WPF forms as they are a more modern solution.
